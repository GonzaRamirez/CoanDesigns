SOV

We strive to completely satisfy our client by adopting a Balinese style for the project. From the signature colors and materials to the careful selection of plants, every detail was inspired by the aesthetics and essence of Bali. From the ground up, we created a comprehensive design that included smoothed concrete wall coverings and sustainable furniture made from reclaimed wood. Each piece, from the sofa to the furniture, was carefully selected or designed to reflect the authenticity and beauty of Balinese style. Additionally, we added a pergola on the terrace, even designing the sofas to perfectly complement the environment. This comprehensive approach ensured a cohesive and immersive experience throughout the space, bringing the essence of Bali to every corner of the home.
