Swell

In this project, we had the opportunity to exercise our creativity freely, which resulted in an amazing experience for our client. Inspired by the Wabi Sabi style, we implemented an approach that emphasizes the beauty of the imperfect and the natural. Every detail, from the furniture to the lamps, was custom designed by CoAn and manufactured by local artisans and artists, ensuring exceptional authenticity and quality throughout the design.
