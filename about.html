Our history

Coan Design

Our trip began by coincidence... they had children in the same school and even in the same grades so they had begun to develop a beautiful friendship. During a conversation, Anahid mentioned that her brother-in-law had bought three condos and wanted her to decorate them since she had already helped decorate his house. He knew she loved design as her father Edward was a fan of design and decoration, and she grew up reading Architectural Digests and Côté Sud magazines, which were always present in her parents' beautifully decorated home.
Conqui with a similar background, her mother being a landscaper and raised in a house full of details, she discovered her passion for interior design. Already with experience in some projects in Sto Dgo and basic design studies, she agreed to accompany Anahid on this great adventure…..The rest is history, as they say, and thus CoAn Designs was born.
The duo began working all over the island; starting with decorating work and now doing remodeling, furniture design, consulting and growing the CoAn Designs team.
Along the way they decided that they wanted to start customizing the furniture they use in their projects and began making tables with organic and sustainable wood. As their experience and clientele grew, they began to feel the need to customize almost everything in their projects: lamps, sofas, all wooden furniture, decorative elements, curtains, etc. This has become CoAn's forte; mixing pieces of natural wood in simple, modern and harmonious spaces.
We love what we do. The dedication, professionalism and passion they put into their work is what made their company grow mainly by word of mouth.
Who knew that a chance conversation between friends 13 years ago would lead to a beautiful collaboration that includes his daughters Karin and María José and other talented young artists to expand their capabilities in architecture and design.
