Following our client's specific request, we developed an urban style that captured the essence of the beach and coastal lifestyle. In the client's words, our goal was to bring the essence of the beach, specifically Cabarete, to her home in the city. We took charge of completely designing the apartment, which involved making significant structural changes, including bathrooms, kitchen and the terrace. Every aspect of the design was inspired by the vibrant coastal atmosphere, ensuring that every corner of the space reflects the energy and relaxation associated with beach living. Additionally, all furniture and lighting was carefully selected from CoAn to complement the overall design and enhance the coastal urban atmosphere we were seeking to achieve.
