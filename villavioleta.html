Villa Violeta

Villa Violeta is a comprehensive design project created by CoAn Designs in collaboration with Cabarete Properties, located within the prestigious Trinitarias development in Cabarete. This villa stands out for its contemporary style, carefully balanced with the tranquility of its tropical surroundings, offering a space that combines modern aesthetics and comfort. Every element of Villa Violeta has been designed with meticulous attention: from the stunning staircase to the lamps, curtains, furniture, and dining table, all crafted by CoAn. The interior design reflects a precise focus on the harmony between functionality and elegance, creating a home that is not only visually appealing but also warm and inviting.
